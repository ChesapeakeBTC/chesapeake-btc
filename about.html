About The Meetup

We gather monthly to educate, participate, and build. Through open discourse, hands-on learning, and active support for local commerce, we help families, individuals, and small businesses throughout Hampton Roads engage with the free and sovereign technologies shaping our future.

We meet on the 4th Saturday of every month at 2pm in Chesapeake, Virginia. All are welcome, no experience required. Our focus is Bitcoin.
